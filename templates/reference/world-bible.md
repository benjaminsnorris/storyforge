## Society & Culture

### Class & Social Structure
- How is society organized? What are the divisions?
- How do people at different levels see each other?
- What mobility exists, and what prevents it?

### Cultural Norms
- What do people value? What do they fear?
- How do they greet each other, resolve disputes, celebrate?
- What is taboo?

### Education & Knowledge
- How is knowledge transmitted? Who has access?
- What has been forgotten, suppressed, or lost?

### Gender, Family, & Relationships
- How are households structured?
- What roles are assumed or contested?

### Other Cultural Notes
- Religion or spirituality (if relevant)
- Art, entertainment, media
- Language, dialect, regional variation

---

## Politics & Power

### Power Structure
- Who holds formal authority? How is it organized?
- Who holds informal authority? Where does real influence live?

### Institutions
For each significant institution:

**[Institution Name]**
- Function and jurisdiction
- Key personnel (or placeholder roles)
- Relationship to other institutions
- How it has been corrupted, weakened, or co-opted (if applicable)

### Laws & Governance
- What legal or charter mechanisms matter to the plot?
- What safeguards exist, and are they functional?

### Political Tensions
- What factions or interests are in conflict?
- What compromises hold the system together, and which are failing?
