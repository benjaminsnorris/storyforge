### Costs & Limits

Every system must cost something. Free power is uninteresting power.

- **What does it cost the user?** (Physical, psychological, temporal, social)
- **How does the cost scale?** (Does greater power cost disproportionately more?)
- **What is the upper limit?** (What happens if the user pushes past it?)
- **Is the cost recoverable?** (If so, how? If not, what is permanently lost?)
