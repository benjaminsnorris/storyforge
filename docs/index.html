Novel-Writing Toolkit for Claude Code

Storyforge

A confident collaborator that combines interactive creative guidance with autonomous manuscript execution. You decide where to work. Storyforge decides how.

The philosophy: Rather than asking endless clarifying questions, Storyforge makes creative decisions, executes them, and produces work for you to review and refine. Three coaching levels let you control how much creative authority Claude holds — from full partnership to strict author control.
What it does
01

Guided Development

Interactive skills for world-building, character work, voice discovery, and scene design. Storyforge challenges weak ideas, suggests alternatives, and builds comprehensive reference documents.

02

Autonomous Drafting

Once your foundation is solid, ./storyforge write drafts scenes autonomously — reading your world bible, character bible, voice guide, and scene index to produce consistent prose.

03

Multi-Persona Evaluation

Six evaluator personas — literary agent, developmental editor, line editor, genre expert, writing coach, and first reader — analyze your manuscript from every angle.

04

Structured Revision

Plan targeted revision passes from evaluation findings. Each pass has a clear purpose, scope, and guidance. Execute autonomously or collaborate interactively — your choice.

05

Book Production

Map scenes to chapters, configure typography with genre presets, and assemble into epub, PDF, or HTML. Every autonomous step ends with an automatic quality review.

06

Coaching Levels

Three modes — full, coach, and strict — let you control how much creative authority Claude holds. From active partner to silent file clerk.
