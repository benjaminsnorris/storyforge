The Elaboration Pipeline

Progressive structural development: spine → architecture → voice → scene map → briefs → draft → polish. Validation at every stage.

Extraction

Reverse elaboration for existing manuscripts. Four phases extract structural data from prose into the three-file scene model.

Scoring & Fidelity

25 craft principles per scene. Fidelity scoring checks if prose delivers what the brief promised. Findings route upstream.

Coaching Levels

Three ways to work: creative partner (full), dramaturg (coach), or continuity editor (strict). Each role is genuinely valuable.
