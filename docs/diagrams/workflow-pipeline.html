Storyforge Pipeline

A novel-writing toolkit for Claude Code. Guides authors from initial concept through drafting, evaluation, revision, and production across thirteen interconnected phases.

Interactive — Author collaborates with Claude
Autonomous — Claude executes, author reviews
1
Init
/storyforge:init
Project Scaffold

Create the project directory structure, configure genre, setting, target word count, and establish the working documents that guide every subsequent phase.

Interactive
2
Develop
/storyforge:develop
World and Story

World-building, character development, story architecture, and timeline construction. Typically spans multiple sessions as the narrative foundation takes shape.

Interactive — Multiple Sessions
3
Voice
/storyforge:voice
Prose Identity

Discover the novel's prose voice through guided exploration. Produces a style guide covering POV rules, sensory palette, rhythm, diction, and tonal boundaries.

Interactive
Iterate
4
Scene Design
/storyforge:scenes
Scene Index

Design the complete scene index. Plan every scene with metadata: characters present, setting, purpose, emotional arc, and structural role within the narrative.

Interactive
5
Draft
./storyforge write
Scene Drafting

Autonomous scene drafting. Claude writes each scene using all reference documents — voice guide, scene plans, character sheets, and world-building notes — as context.

Autonomous
6
Evaluate
./storyforge evaluate
Manuscript Analysis

A six-persona evaluation panel analyzes the manuscript. Each persona assesses different dimensions — prose quality, pacing, character consistency, plot coherence, and more.

Autonomous
7
Plan Revision
/storyforge:plan-revision
Revision Strategy

Author and Claude review evaluation findings together. Prioritize issues, define revision passes, and create a concrete plan before any changes are made to the manuscript.

Interactive
8
Revise
./storyforge revise
Manuscript Revision

Execute the planned revision passes. Combines autonomous revisions — where Claude applies systematic fixes — with interactive passes for nuanced editorial decisions.

Autonomous + Interactive
Iterative Refinement

Steps 4 through 8 form a refinement loop. After revision, return to Scene Design to restructure, then draft, evaluate, and revise again. Each cycle sharpens the manuscript toward its final form.

Scene Design → Draft → Evaluate → Plan Revision → Revise
9
Review
/storyforge:review
Quality Gate

Automatic review runs at the end of every autonomous process — write, evaluate, revise, and assemble. Maps findings to changes and identifies gaps. Delegates to /storyforge:recommend for next-step decisions. Also available as a standalone skill.

Automatic + Interactive
10
Title
/storyforge:title
Title Development

Develop, refine, or finalize the book title and subtitle. Can be invoked at any phase, but naturally fits here — right before the book becomes a real artifact.

Interactive
11
Cover
/storyforge:cover
Cover Design

Design a custom book cover. Claude composes sophisticated SVG artwork from the story's themes and imagery, or generates AI illustrations with text compositing. Always iterative — preview, refine, approve.

Interactive
12
Produce
/storyforge:produce
Book Assembly

Map scenes to chapters, configure typography and production settings, then assemble into epub, PDF, or HTML. Chapter mapping is a creative act — shaped by coaching level.

Interactive + Autonomous
13
Press Kit
/storyforge:press-kit
Marketing Materials

Generate blurbs, jacket copy, author bio, social media teasers, selling points, comp titles, and query pitch. Everything an author needs to present their book to the world.

Interactive
