The Evaluation Panel

Six specialist personas read your manuscript independently, each bringing a distinct editorial lens. Their structured findings are synthesized into a single actionable report.

./storyforge evaluate

The Panel

Six editorial perspectives

Literary Agent

  • Hook strength and elevator pitch

  • Commercial positioning

  • Where attention drifts

Process

From draft to findings

1

Draft

Assembled chapters are loaded from the manuscript

2

Panel Analysis

Six personas evaluate independently in parallel

3

Reports

Each evaluator produces a structured report

4

Synthesis

Findings are merged and cross-referenced

5

Findings

Actionable findings.yaml ready for review

Output

Evaluation artifacts

File structure

working/evaluations/
  findings.yaml # merged findings
  synthesis.md # narrative assessment
  [per-evaluator]/ # detailed reports

Finding severity levels

Critical Fundamental issues that undermine the work
Major Significant problems that weaken impact
Minor Small issues worth addressing in revision
Suggestion Optional enhancements to consider

What happens next

After reviewing the evaluation findings, run /storyforge:plan-revision to triage the results. The revision planner reads findings.yaml, lets you decide which issues to address, and produces a structured revision plan with concrete steps for each chapter.
