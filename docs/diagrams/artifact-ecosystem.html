Artifact Reference
World Bible
world-bible.md
Develop
  • Geography, climate, and physical setting
  • History, culture, and societal structures
  • Technology level and world rules
  • Sensory and atmospheric details
Created by: /storyforge:develop
Consumed by: Writing, Evaluation, Revision
Character Bible
character-bible.md
Develop
  • Named characters with appearance and psychology
  • Voice patterns and speech mannerisms
  • Relationship maps and dynamics
  • Character arcs and growth trajectories
Created by: /storyforge:develop
Consumed by: Voice, Scene Design, Writing, Evaluation
Systems Bible
systems-bible.md
Develop
  • Invented systems: magic, technology, etc.
  • Rules, costs, and hard constraints
  • Interactions between systems
  • Known exceptions and edge cases
Created by: /storyforge:develop
Consumed by: Writing, Evaluation, Revision
Story Architecture
story-architecture.md
Develop
  • Act structure and turning points
  • Subplot threads and intersections
  • Thematic throughlines
  • Pacing targets and tension curve
Created by: /storyforge:develop
Consumed by: Scene Design, Writing, Evaluation
Timeline
timeline.md
Develop
  • Chronological event log
  • Temporal consistency reference
  • Parallel thread synchronization
  • Backstory and future event anchors
Created by: /storyforge:develop
Consumed by: Scene Design, Writing, Evaluation
Voice Guide
voice-guide.md
Voice
  • POV strategy and narrative distance
  • Prose rhythm and sentence patterns
  • Dialogue philosophy and conventions
  • Style rules and forbidden patterns
Created by: /storyforge:voice
Informed by: Character Bible (POV voices)
Consumed by: Writing, Evaluation, Revision
Scene Index
scene-index.yaml
Scenes
  • Master list of every scene with metadata
  • POV, setting, and characters per scene
  • Scene function, emotional arc, threads
  • Target word counts
Created by: /storyforge:scenes
Informed by: Story Architecture, Character Bible, Timeline
Consumed by: Writing, Evaluation
Continuity Tracker
continuity-tracker.md
Ongoing
  • Living ledger of continuity facts
  • Promises made to the reader
  • Open and resolved threads
  • Cross-scene consistency checks
Created by: /storyforge:develop (initial)
Updated by: All subsequent phases
Consumed by: Writing, Evaluation, Revision
