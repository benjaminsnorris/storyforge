The Craft Engine

Two layers of craft knowledge — structural principles that validate before drafting, and prose principles that score after drafting.

Structural Craft

Tracked in the scene CSVs. Validated automatically. Problems caught before prose exists.

Value Shifts (McKee)

Every scene must change a value's polarity. Flat stretches of 3+ scenes are flagged. The value shift arc visualizes the story's emotional trajectory.

Scene Rhythm (Swain)

Action scenes (goal/conflict/outcome) alternate with sequel scenes (reaction/dilemma/decision). Four consecutive same type is flagged as a pacing problem.

Five Commandments (Story Grid)

Inciting incident, progressive complications, crisis, climax, resolution. The crisis must be a genuine dilemma — best bad choice or irreconcilable goods.

MICE Threads (Kowal)

Milieu, inquiry, character, event threads with FILO nesting. Validation catches nesting violations, orphaned threads, and dormancy beyond 8 scenes.
