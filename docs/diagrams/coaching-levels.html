Three Ways to Work

Coaching levels determine Claude's role — not how much help, but what kind.

Full

Creative Partner

Claude proposes the spine, builds architecture, writes briefs, drafts scenes. The author reviews at PR gates.

Could be surprised by creative choices — structural guardrails ensure quality even with less oversight.

Coach

Dramaturg

Claude presents options and asks questions. The author makes every creative decision; Claude structures and challenges.

Strict

Continuity Editor

The author writes everything. Claude runs exhaustive structural validation — the kind of checking that's genuinely hard for humans across 50+ scenes.
