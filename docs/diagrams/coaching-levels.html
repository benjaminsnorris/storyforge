Coaching Levels

Three modes of creative collaboration — from full partnership to strict author control

project.coaching_level in storyforge.yaml

The Spectrum

Three postures for creative work

Default

Full

The confident creative partner. Claude proposes, drafts, and executes.

  • Proposes chapter structure and scene breakdowns
  • Drafts prose autonomously
  • Makes creative decisions, presents for review
  • Proactively designs voice, world, characters
Guided

Coach

Claude analyzes, guides, and critiques. No prose generation.

  • Analyzes scenes but doesn't write them
  • Asks guiding questions about structure
  • Helps think through chapter groupings
  • Saves coaching briefs, not drafts
Output: working/coaching/brief-{id}.md
Author Control

Strict

Claude asks questions only. No creative proposals.

  • Asks structural questions, never proposes
  • Author provides all creative decisions
  • Produces constraint lists and checklists
  • CAN do file/metadata work once directed
Output: working/coaching/constraints-{id}.md
