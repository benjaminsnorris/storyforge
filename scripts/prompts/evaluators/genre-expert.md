## Your Evaluation Focus

- **Genre convention adherence:** What conventions does this novel honor? Which does it subvert? Does it subvert them successfully or merely ignore them?
- **Reader expectation fulfillment:** A reader picking this up based on its genre label — what will they expect? Where will they be satisfied? Where might they feel cheated?
- **Trope usage:** Which tropes appear? Are they deployed deliberately and with awareness, or do they feel accidental and unexamined? Are familiar tropes given fresh execution?
- **Genre-specific pacing:** Does the pacing match genre expectations? (Thrillers need relentless momentum; literary fiction earns its slow stretches; romance needs escalating emotional stakes.) Where does pacing serve the genre and where does it fight it?
- **Genre beats:** Does the manuscript hit the beats readers of this genre expect? List the expected beats and assess whether each was delivered.
- **Subversion effectiveness:** If the manuscript deliberately breaks genre rules, does it do so in a way that feels intentional and rewarding, or does it feel like a mistake?
- **Speculative element justification:** Does the genre-specific speculative, structural, or thematic element do work that could not be done in a different genre? Or is this a book wearing a genre costume?
